- Introducing the Document Scanning Apps#

Today, we are living in a world where technology is making our lives easier. Document scanning apps for Android offer a great way to quickly and easily scan any kind of documents, including images and photos. With these apps, you can quickly capture any document and convert it to a PDF. Many of these apps also offer a built-in OCR feature, which allows you to extract text from the scan. In this guide, we will look at the best document scanning apps for Android.

Scanbot is an easy-to-use document scanning app that offers a wide range of features, including automatic edge detection, perspective correction, and file sharing. It also offers a fast OCR engine to convert scans to text, and a powerful document search feature. Scanbot is available for free with in-app purchases for additional features.

CamScanner is one of the most popular document scanning apps on Android. It offers an easy-to-use interface, automatic edge detection, and a handy document sharing feature. With CamScanner, you can quickly and easily scan any kind of documents and convert them to PDF, JPG, or PNG format.

Microsoft Office Lens is a free document scanning app designed for both Android and iOS devices. It offers an easy-to-use interface, automatic edge detection, and optical character recognition (OCR) for text extraction. It also allows you to export your scans to your Microsoft account for further processing.

Google Drive is Google’s cloud storage service, and it also includes a document scanning app. It offers an easy-to-use interface, automatic edge detection, and even a text recognition feature for extracting text from images. You can also easily share your scans via Google Drive.

- Installing a Document Scanner App#

Scanning documents with your Android device has never been easier. All the major Android brands have their own document scanning apps that you can download from the Play Store. With these apps, you can quickly and easily turn physical documents into digital ones. Once you have a digital copy, you can easily email, print or upload it to cloud storage services. Here’s how to scan a document on an Android device:

First, download and install the document scanning app of your choice. Once you’ve opened the app, you’ll need to align and frame the document you’d like to scan. Make sure that you’ve got the correct orientation, lighting and contrast set up. When you’re ready, tap the ‘scan’ button in the app to take a picture of the document.

The app will then process the image and make it look more legible. It will also save the scanned image to your device. To make further edits, you can select the ‘edit’ option. This will bring up a host of options, such as cropping, rotating and converting the image to a PDF.

Finally, you can save the scanned document to your device or share it with someone else. Many of the scanning apps also have cloud integration so you can save the document to a cloud storage service like Dropbox or Google Drive.

That’s how to scan a document on an Android device. With the right app, the process is simple and straightforward. In no time, you’ll be able to scan, edit and share documents with just a few taps.

2. Set Up Document Scanner App#

Scanning documents on an Android phone is easy. Here are the steps to follow to get it done. First, make sure your phone is connected to a printer with scanning capabilities. Then, open the camera app on your phone and locate the settings icon. Tap on the gear icon and select Scan a document. Now, you should be seeing a document scanner similar to the one found on PC scanners. Place your document on the printer’s document tray and select the Scan button. Your document will be scanned and saved in your phone’s photo gallery. Finally, you can email the scanned document or save the document to cloud storage.
