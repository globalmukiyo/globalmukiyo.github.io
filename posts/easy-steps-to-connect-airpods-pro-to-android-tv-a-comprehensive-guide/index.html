1. What you need#

In order to connect AirPods Pro to your Android TV, you’ll need an Android TV box with USB-C port, a USB-C to 3.5mm headphone jack dongle, and, of course, your AirPods Pro. Make sure you’ve got all the pieces you need to make the connection, then follow the steps outlined in this guide and you’ll be connected in no time. Firstly, ensure that both your Android TV and AirPods Pro are turned on and are within Bluetooth range of each other. Then, plug the USB-C dongle into the USB-C port on the TV, and the headphone jack dongle into the AirPods Pro. Once the two devices are connected, they will automatically pair with each other. Lastly, you’ll need to adjust the audio settings on your Android TV to ensure that the sound coming through your headset is at the desired level. With that done, you’re all set with your AirPods Pro connected to your Android TV!

2. Steps to Connecting Airpods Pro#

To connect AirPods Pro to your Android TV, you will need the AirPods Pro charging case, a USB-C cable, and an Android TV that supports Bluetooth. First, open the charging case and make sure the AirPods Pro’s LED light is in contact with the case. Next, connect the USB-C cable to the charging case and your Android TV. Finally, open the Bluetooth settings on the Android TV and select AirPods Pro from the available devices. That’s it! Now you know how to connect AirPods Pro to your Android TV.

2. Setup process#

Setting up AirPods Pro to an Android TV is an easy process. First, ensure that the AirPods Pro are powered on and the LED light is blinking. Then, open the Settings tab on the Android TV. Select the Bluetooth option and click on the Add Bluetooth device button. The Android TV will search for nearby Bluetooth devices, and should locate the AirPods Pro. Select the device to pair it with the Android TV. The AirPods Pro should now be connected to the Android TV and ready for use. If the device isn’t connecting, try rebooting the AirPods Pro and the TV. This should help the devices pair up. Lastly, make sure the volume settings are adjusted to a comfortable level. That’s it!
