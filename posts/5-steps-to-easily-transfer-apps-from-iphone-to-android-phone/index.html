2. Guide on How to Transfer Apps from iPhone to Android Phone#

With more people using both iOS and Android devices, it is increasingly important to know how to transfer apps from an iPhone to an Android phone. Here are five easy steps that will help you move your apps to a different device:

Step 1: Download Google Drive on both your iPhone and Android phone. This will allow you to transfer the data from your iPhone to your Android device.

Step 2: Open the Google Drive app on your iPhone and tap on the ‘Upload’ button. Select the apps you wish to transfer then click ‘Upload’.

Step 3: Open Google Drive on your Android device and select ‘My Drive’. Tap on the ‘Uploads’ folder and select the apps you wish to install.

Step 4: Open the Google Play store on your Android device and select ‘My Apps’. Tap on the ‘New’ button and select the apps you wish to install.

Step 5: Finally, tap the ‘Install’ button on your Android device to install the apps you transferred from your iPhone.

By following these steps, you can easily transfer apps from an iPhone to an Android phone. Just make sure that you have downloaded and installed the Google Drive app on both your iPhone and Android device.
