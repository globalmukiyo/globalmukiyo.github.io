1. How to Access the Screenshot Tool on iPad Air 4th Gen#

If you wish to edit the screenshot, you can easily do so by tapping on the ‘Edit’ button when viewing the screenshot in the Photos app. You can also crop, rotate, and add filters and other effects to the screenshot.

That’s all there is to taking a screenshot on your iPad Air 4th Gen. With this simple guide, you can capture any screen you like with ease.
