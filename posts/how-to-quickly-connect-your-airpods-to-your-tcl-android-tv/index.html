1. Check System Compatibility#

Before connecting your Airpods to your TCL Android TV, it is important to ensure that your system is compatible with Airpod’s Bluetooth connection. Once you have verified the system compatibility, you will need to ensure that the TV is in the Bluetooth pairing mode. You can find this in the Bluetooth option of the TV’s menu. You can also activate the pairing mode on the TV remote by pressing the pairing button.
