1. Prepare to Transfer WhatsApp Chats from Android to iPhone#

Transferring WhatsApp chats from Android to iPhone is no easy task, but fortunately there is a way to do it with Google Drive. In this tutorial, we’ll teach you how to transfer WhatsApp chats from Android to iPhone in just a few simple steps.

First, on your Android device, open the WhatsApp app and go to the Settings tab. Then, select Chats followed by Chat History. Finally, tap on the Back Up button to back up your WhatsApp chats to Google Drive.

On your iPhone device, install the WhatsApp app and then sign in with the same phone number as used on your Android device. Then, go to the Settings tab and select Chats. Here, tap on Chat Backup and then tap on Restore Backup to begin the transfer process.

Once the transfer is complete, all of your WhatsApp chats will be transferred from your Android device to your iPhone. Follow these simple steps and you’ll have your WhatsApp chats transferred between devices in no time.

Q1. How do I transfer my WhatsApp chats from Android to iPhone using Google Drive?

A1. Transferring WhatsApp chats from Android to iPhone with Google Drive is a simple process. First, make sure you have a Google Drive account and that it is already linked to your device. Then, open WhatsApp on your Android phone and go to the Chats tab. Long press on the chat you want to transfer and tap on “More”, then select “Export chat”. Choose “Google Drive” as the backup option and follow the on-screen prompts to complete the transfer. Once completed, open up the Google Drive app on your iPhone and sign in with your Google account, then locate the backup file you’ve created and tap to restore it. Your Whatsapp chats will be restored to your new iPhone.

On your iPhone, open the Google Drive app and sign in with your Google account, then locate the backup file created and tap to restore it. Your WhatsApp contacts will be restored to your new iPhone.

A4. When you transfer chats from your Android to your iPhone and vice versa, the existing chat information on both devices will be replaced by the data you’ve transferred.
