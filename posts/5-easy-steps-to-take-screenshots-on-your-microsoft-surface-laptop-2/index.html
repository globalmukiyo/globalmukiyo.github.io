1. Understand the Keyboard shortcut for taking screenshots#

Taking screenshots on your Microsoft Surface Laptop 2 is very easy and convenient if you understand the keyboard shortcut for it. To take a screenshot, simply press and hold the Windows logo button and the volume-down button at the same time. The screen will dim momentarily, and the screenshot is saved to the Pictures folder in your Surface. You can also rename it if you want.

If you want to take a screenshot of active window, press and hold the Windows logo button, then press PrtScn (Print Screen) button. The screenshot will be saved to the Pictures>Screenshots folder. You can also access this folder from the File Explorer.

For taking a partial screenshot, press and hold the Windows logo and Shift buttons, and then press the S button. Then you can select the part of your screen that you want to take a screenshot of. You can also drag the corners of the screenshot to resize it.

Finally, if you want to take a screenshot of an app window that you don’t want to select, press and hold the Alt key and press the PrtScn button. This will take a screenshot of the active app window only and save it to the Pictures>Screenshots folder.
