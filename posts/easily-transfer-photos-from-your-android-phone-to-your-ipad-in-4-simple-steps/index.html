Before starting the transfer process, make sure both your devices, the Android phone and the Apple iPad, have power and are turned on. 1. Prepare the Devices#

Establishing a connection between devices is the easy part. To begin transferring photos from your Android phone to an Apple iPad, make sure that both devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Next, open the Settings app on your Android device and select Wi-Fi connection status. Go to the Wi-Fi network list and check to make sure your iPad is listed. If it is not, tap the + sign to add it. You can now connect your Android and iPad devices.

To transfer photos, use the Files by Google app on your Android device. Open the app and select Send and Receive, then select the iPhone/iPad icon. You should now be prompted to enter your Apple ID and password. Once you have done so, select Allow Access and you will be able to send photos to your iPad.

On your iPad, open the Files App and select Receive Files from the menu. You should now be able to access the photos stored on your Android device. Select the photos that you want to transfer and then tap the Send button. The photos will now be transferred to your iPad.

Lastly, disconnect your iPad and Android device from the Wi-Fi network. The photos you have transferred will now be saved on your iPad. With these simple steps, you can easily transfer photos from your Android phone to your Apple iPad.
