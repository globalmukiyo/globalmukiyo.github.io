1. Introduction to WhatsApp Voice Messages#

WhatsApp has a great feature that allows you to send and receive voice messages. With this feature, you can easily send your friends and family messages without having to type them out. However, if you want to keep these messages, you need to learn how to save them. Here’s a tutorial on how to easily save your voice messages from WhatsApp on your Android device.

First, open WhatsApp and tap the conversation where the voice message is located. Then, press and hold the voice message until a menu appears with the available options. Choose the ‘Save’ option and it will be saved onto your device’s memory.

Next, open the file Explorer on your Android device and go to the ‘WhatsApp’ folder. Inside the folder, you will find a ‘Voice Messages’ subfolder. Your audio file will be saved in this folder.

Finally, if you want to play the voice message in another audio player, you can copy the file and paste it in another folder. To rename the file, hold and press it until the rename option appears. Then, rename it to your desired name.

Q: Where can I find the saved voice messages on my Android device? A: The saved voice messages can be found in the ‘WhatsApp Audio’ folder in the internal storage of your Android device.

Q: Is it possible to backup the voice messages from WhatsApp on my Android device? A: Yes, it is possible to backup the voice messages from WhatsApp on your Android device. Select ‘Backup to Google Drive’ from the menu and the voice message will be backed up to your Google Drive account.
