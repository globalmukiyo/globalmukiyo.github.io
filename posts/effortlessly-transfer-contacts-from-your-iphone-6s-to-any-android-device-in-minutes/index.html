2. Steps for Transferring Contacts#

Transferring contacts from an iPhone 6s to an Android device is becoming more and more common. However, it can be tricky if you don’t know the steps. Here are some tips to help you make sure the transfer goes smoothly.

The first step is to back up your current contacts on your iPhone 6s. You can do this by going to Settings > iCloud > Storage & Backup > Backup Now. This will save a copy of your contacts onto Apple’s servers.

Secondly, you need to export your contacts to a format which is compatible with Android devices. To do this, open the Contacts app on your iPhone 6s and select ‘Groups’. Tap the ‘All iCloud’ option and then choose ‘Export vCard’.

Thirdly, you must locate the exported vCard file on your computer. It should be in your ‘Downloads’ folder. To ensure that it imports correctly into your Android device, rename it with the .vcf file extension, for example contacts.vcf.

Finally, transfer the vCard file to your Android device. You can do this using a USB cable, cloud storage services such as Dropbox or Google Drive, or an email attachment. Once the transfer is complete, open the People app on your Android device and select the ‘Menu’ icon. Tap ‘Settings’, then select ‘Import contacts’ and choose the vCard file. Your contacts will now be imported and will appear in the People app.
