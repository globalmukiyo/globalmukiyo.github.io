1. Preparation before transferring data#

Before you can transfer data from an old Android phone to a new one, there are some preparations that need to be done. Firstly, ensure a stable connection between both phones. Backup any important data that you may have stored on the old phone such as contacts, images, and other documents. Ensure that all data you need is stored in the cloud and can be accessed from the new phone. Make sure both phones have enough storage space to accommodate the data that is being transferred. Lastly, switch off both phones and enable airplane mode. This will ensure that no data is lost and reduce the risk of network interference. By following these steps, you will be able to easily transfer data from an old to a new Android phone.

2. Steps to Successfully Transfer Data#

Before starting the process of transferring data from an old and broken phone to a new phone, it is important to make some preparations. Firstly, establish a WiFi connection between the two devices. This will ensure that the transfer process runs smoothly and without interruption. Secondly, make sure to fully charge both phones to prevent the process from being disrupted due to power shortage. Thirdly, make a backup of the Android phone to be transferred using Google Drive, or any other cloud storage, in order to make sure that no data is lost during the transfer. Lastly, create an account in the new Android phone to facilitate the transfer of data. The steps above are important as they set the groundwork for the successful transfer of data from an old and broken phone to a new phone.
