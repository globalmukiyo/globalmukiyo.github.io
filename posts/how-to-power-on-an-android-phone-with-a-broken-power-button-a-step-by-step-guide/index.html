Powering on an Android phone with a broken power button can be a difficult ordeal. Luckily, Android has several solutions to this problem. The first one is to use Smart Lock. Smart Lock is a feature that was first added to Android 5.0 Lollipop that allows you to bypass the lock screen if you have a device in close proximity to the phone. To use Smart Lock on Android devices, go to Settings > Security > Smart Lock and set up a secure device or location. Once setup, you simply need to bring your device close to the phone to power it on.

• Steps of Using Smart Lock#

Smart Lock is a great way to turn on any android phone with a broken power button. First, you need to activate the Smart Lock feature on your phone. To do this, go to Settings > Security > Smart Lock. After you’ve entered your PIN or pattern, tap Trusted face or Trusted voice to unlock your phone with your face or voice. Once this is done, you’ll be able to use Smart Lock to turn on your phone even with a broken power button. You just need to turn on your phone’s proximity sensor by tapping the power button for a long press, then your face or voice will be recognized and the phone will be unlocked.

Q1 : How do I power on my Android phone with a broken power button?

Connect one end of the cable to your phone’s charging port and the other end to a power source like a laptop or powerbank. Your phone should turn on automatically after a few seconds.

Q2: Is there any other way to power on my Android phone with a broken power button?

A2 : Yes, if you have an NFC tag, you can enable a feature called Android Beam which will allow you to power on your phone when you tap it against the tag. You can find instructions for enabling Android Beam in your phone’s settings.

Q3 : What is USB On-The-Go (OTG) cable?

A3 : USB On-The-Go (OTG) cable is a special type of USB cable that can be used to connect two USB devices together. For example, you can use it to connect your Android phone to a computer, or to connect a USB drive or external keyboard to your phone.

Q4: What types of power sources can I use to power on my Android phone?

A4 : You can use any of the following power sources to power on your Android phone: a laptop or desktop computer, a powerbank, or a USB wall adapter.

Q5 : What should I do if my Android phone still won’t power on after connecting it to a power source?

A5 : If your phone still won’t power on after connecting it to a power source, you may need to perform a factory reset. You can usually find instructions for factory resetting your phone in your device’s manual or in the settings menu.
