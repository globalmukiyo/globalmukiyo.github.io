A2: To enable USB debugging, you will need to go to the Settings menu, then Developer Options. From there, you will need to enable the USB Debugging toggle switch and confirm your choice.

Q3: Is there a certain type of USB cable I need to use for the installation process?

A3: Yes, you will need to use a USB cable that is compatible with your device and PC. If you are unsure, you can double check the compatibility list before purchasing a USB cable.

A4: After you have enabled USB debugging and connected the device with the PC via the USB cable, you will need to download the Android OS ROM and move it to the device’s internal storage.
