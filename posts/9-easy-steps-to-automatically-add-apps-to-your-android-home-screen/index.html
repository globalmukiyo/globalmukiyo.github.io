Next, let’s take a look at how to automatically add apps to your Android home screen. By default, all the apps installed on your device will be on the home screen. Uninstalled apps will still remain on the home screen, but changing the settings will let you automate the process.

To do this, open your device’s settings, go to the ‘Apps’ menu and tap on the ‘App Drawer Settings’ option. Here you will be able to find the ‘Auto add to Home Screen’ option. Switch this option on, and every app that you install from then on will be added to the home screen automatically.

Q1 : How do I add apps to my Android home screen?

A1 : You can add apps to your Android home screen by following these nine easy steps:

  1. Open the Google Play store app on your device.
  2. Search for the app you want to add to your home screen.
  3. Tap on the app you want to add.
  4. Tap the install button on the app page.
  5. Once the installation is finished, open the app.
  6. Tap and hold the app icon.
  7. Drag the app icon on to your home screen.
  8. Drop the app icon onto the home screen.
  9. You have now successfully added the app icon to the home screen.

  4. The app or widget icon will disappear from the home screen.
