1. How to Access the Android’s Recovery Menu#

If you’re looking to reset and factory reset your Android device, you can do so by accessing the recovery menu using a few simple buttons. This guide will walk you through each step of the process.

First, enter into recovery mode on your Android device by pressing and holding the power button and volume down button simultaneously, usually for about 5-7 seconds. A menu will appear with a few options.

Select the “wipe data/factory reset” option, which should allow you to reset your device back to its original state. This menu should also give you options to “wipe cache partition” and “apply update from ADB.”

Confirm your action and the device should start resetting itself. After the process has completed, you should have a freshly wiped device.

Finally, restart your device and, if the process has been successful, your device should boot up as if it were brand new. Now you are ready to start the setup process again from scratch, without all the clutter of old files and apps.
