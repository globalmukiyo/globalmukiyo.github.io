Here are the steps to follow in order to take a screenshot on a Dell Chromebook touchscreen.

Firstly, press the Power Button and Volume Down Button at the same time. This will capture the screenshot and it will be saved in the “Downloads” folder of the Chromebook by default. You can also use the keyboard shortcut “Ctrl + F5” to quickly take a screenshot.

Secondly, you can adjust the screenshot settings to your preferences. To do this, open the “Settings” menu and select the “Display” tab. From here, you will be able to change the “Resolution”, set a specific area to capture, or change the “Screenshot Delay”.

Thirdly, you can also use a stylus to take a screenshot on the Dell Chromebook touchscreen. To do this, tap on the stylus button and then press down on the screen to select the area you want to capture. The screenshot will then be saved in the “Downloads” folder of the Chromebook.

Lastly, you can share the screenshot to other people by using the Chromebook’s “Share” feature. To do this, simply open the “Files” app and locate the screenshot file. Then click on the “Share” icon and select the person or people you want to share it with.

By following these steps, you can easily take a screenshot on your Dell Chromebook touchscreen. This makes it much easier to capture images, documents, or anything else you need to save and share.
