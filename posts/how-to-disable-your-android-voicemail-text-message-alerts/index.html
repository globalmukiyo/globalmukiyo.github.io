1. Ways To Disable Your Voicemail Text Message Alerts#

Many people are unaware that their Android phones can be configured to disable voicemail text message alerts. This can be a great feature for those who don’t want to be disturbed by notifications. Here are three simple steps to disable your Android voicemail text message alerts:

Firstly, open the voicemail app on your Android and locate the Settings menu. Select the General tab and then navigate to the Notifications section. Uncheck the box labeled Show notifications. This will stop any voicemail alert notifications from appearing on your phone.

Secondly, go to the Call Settings tab on your Android phone. Then, press the Voicemail option and select Notifications. Uncheck the box labeled Enable notifications. This will prevent any voicemail alert notifications from appearing, even when you receive a new message.

Finally, if you don’t want to completely disable your voicemail notifications, you can customize how they appear on your device. To do this, navigate to the Notifications tab, then select the Customize option. Here you can specify the type of alert you would like to receive when you receive a voicemail. You can choose from a specific sound, vibration, or even a popup message.

By following these three steps, you can easily disable your Android voicemail text message alerts. This can be especially useful for those who don’t want to be disturbed by notifications.

These are four ways to turn off voicemail text message alert on your Android device so you can enjoy some peace and quiet.
