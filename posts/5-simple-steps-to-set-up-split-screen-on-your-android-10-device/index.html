Are you using an Android 10 device? Looking for ways to set up your device for split-screen mode? Here are the five simplest steps to get you started with split-screen mode quickly and easily. As technology advances, so does the ability to enjoy our days without a hitch. One of the great accomplishments of modern-day technology is the capability to split-screen our Android 10 device. With the simple steps below, you can easily set up split screen on your Android 10 device, and it will be a breeze.

First, press the “Recent” button on the bottom right corner of the device’s home screen. Then, you will see two windows side-by-side. From here, you can choose the apps you wish to open in the split view. The apps will open simultaneously, and the split view will become active.

Second, you will need to drag the window divider to your desired location. This will allow you to adjust the window size to your preference. When you have reached the desired window size, the split view will be ready for use.

Third, to move an app to the other window in the split view, simply hold and drag it to the other side. This will move the app to the other window.

Fourth, you can make adjustments to each window in the split view. Tap the menu icon in the top-right corner of each window to access its options. You can adjust the size of the window, rotate its orientation, and even add a taskbar.

Finally, to exit the split view, simply drag the divider all the way to the top or bottom of the screen. This will exit the split view, and your device will return to its original state.

By following these five simple steps, you can easily set up a split view on your Android 10 device with ease. With a split view, you can explore and multitask comfortably, with the ultimate convenience of modern technology.

1. Understand Split Screen on Android 10#

Split Screen is a feature introduced to Android users on Android 10, allowing users to open and view multiple apps simultaneously on their device. This feature can be quite useful for multitasking and getting more done in less time. Here are 5 simple steps to set up Split Screen on your Android 10 device.

  1. The first step is to open the Overview (multi-tasking panel) by swiping up your Home Screen.
  2. Select the two apps that you wish to view simultaneously. You can choose two different apps or two of the same if needed.
  3. After selecting the apps, drag the divider of one apps to the top or bottom of the screen.
  4. Finally, press and hold the recent apps button on the navigation bar and the two apps will be split. Now both apps can be viewed on the screen at the same time.

Following these 5 simple steps will help you to easily set up split screen on your Android 10 device. It is a great way to multitask and save time.
