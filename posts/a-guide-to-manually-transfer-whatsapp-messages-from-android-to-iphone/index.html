1. Prerequisites To Manually Transfer WhatsApp Messages From Android To iPhone#

Before starting the process of transferring WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone, there are few prerequisites that need to be met. Firstly, both the Android and iPhone must be compatible with the latest version of WhatsApp. Secondly, make sure that the Android device is backed up with the Google Drive and the iPhone is logged into iCloud. Thirdly, make sure that both the devices have enough storage space available or else the transfer process might encounter errors. 2. Checklist Before Manual Transferring WhatsApp Messages From Android To iPhone.#

Manually transferring WhatsApp messages from an Android device to an iPhone device can be done in a few simple steps. Before beginning, you should make sure that you have the following items: an Android phone, an iPhone, a computer, and an internet connection.

The first step is to create a backup of your messages on your Android device. This can be done by navigating to WhatsApp Settings, tapping Chat Settings and then tapping Chat Backup. Once the backup process is complete, you can connect your Android device to your computer and transfer the backup file to it.

The second step involves connecting your iPhone to your computer and launching iTunes. Once iTunes is open, you should select your device from the top left corner and click “Back Up Now”. After the backup process is complete, you can disconnect your iPhone.

The third step is to install WhatsApp on your iPhone. After the installation process is complete, you should launch the app and log into your account with your phone number. Once you’re logged in, you should tap the “Restore Chat History” option. Finally, you should select the backup file from your computer and wait for the messages to be transferred to your iPhone.
