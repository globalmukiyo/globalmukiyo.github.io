2. Steps to Capture Screenshots#

Capturing screenshots on your Dell Chromebook can be extremely useful for a variety of situations. For example, you may need to share a screenshot with tech support, or you may want to save an image or web page for later use. Fortunately, with a few simple steps, you can quickly and easily capture screenshots on your Dell Chromebook. Here’s what you need to do:

  1. Press the “Ctrl” key and the “Switch Window” key at the same time. This will take a screenshot of your entire screen. The “Switch Window” key is the one with the box icon, and it’s located between the “Ctrl” and “Alt” keys.

  2. To capture only a portion of your screen, press “Ctrl” and the “Shift” key at the same time. Your cursor will turn into a cross-hair. Then, use your mouse to select the portion of the screen that you’d like to capture.

  3. When you’re finished drawing or selecting the area you’d like to capture, press “Ctrl” and “Shift” again. This will take a screenshot of the area you’ve selected.

  4. Your screenshots will be saved in the “Downloads” folder. From here, you can easily move, share, or delete your screenshots.

With these steps, you can easily capture screenshots on your Dell Chromebook.
