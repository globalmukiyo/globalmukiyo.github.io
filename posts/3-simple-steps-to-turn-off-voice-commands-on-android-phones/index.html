Second, go back to your phone’s main settings app. From here, tap “Security & Lock” and then tap on “App Permissions.” In the App Permissions page, scroll down until you find the “Voice Commands” permission. Tap on the toggle to switch it to “Off”.

Finally, if you want to make sure that voice commands are disabled on your phone, open the built-in Google app and tap the three-dot button on the top right corner.
