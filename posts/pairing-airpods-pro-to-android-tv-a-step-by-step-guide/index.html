A3: To pair AirPods Pro with an Android TV, you will need the AirPods Pro, an Android TV, and a Bluetooth connection.

A4: No, there are no risks associated with pairing AirPods Pro with an Android TV. However, as with any Bluetooth pairing, there is always the potential for audio interference, so it is best to ensure you are in a quiet area with no other Bluetooth devices in the vicinity.
