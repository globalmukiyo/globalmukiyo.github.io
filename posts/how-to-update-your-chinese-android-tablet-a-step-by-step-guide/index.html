2. Download the Latest Version of Android Firmware#

Updating your Chinese Android tablet can be a daunting task for even the most experienced tech enthusiasts. With this article, we provide a step-by-step guide to help you quickly and easily update your tablet to the latest version of Android firmware. Here’s what you need to do:

  1. First, you must download the latest version of the Android ROM for your tablet model. You can find this on most Android forums. Once you have downloaded the ZIP file, extract it to your desktop.

  2. The next step is to connect your tablet to your computer using its USB cable. When you see the ‘USB Connected’ notification, open the extracted folder and run the SP Flash tool. This will install the latest ROM onto your device.

  3. Once the installation is complete, disconnect your tablet from your computer and then turn it off. Now you must boot into recovery mode. To do this, press and hold the volume up and power buttons simultaneously until you see the recovery mode menu appear.

  4. When you see the recovery mode menu, select ‘Install zip from SD card’. Navigate to the location of the ROM file and then select it to begin the installation process. Once the installation is complete, reboot your device and enjoy the latest version of Android firmware.
