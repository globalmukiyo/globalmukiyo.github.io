Q2. What types of data can be transferred with Move to iOS?

A2. The Move to iOS app can transfer contacts, message history, photos, videos, web bookmarks, mail accounts, calendars and more. It does not transfer music, apps, or other content purchased from Google Play.

Q3. How do I get the Move to iOS app?

A3. The Move to iOS app is available for free on the Google Play Store. To download it, open the Google Play Store app on your Android device, search for “Move to iOS”, and then tap Install.

Q4. Do I need to set up my iPhone before using Move to iOS?

A4. Yes, you must set up your iPhone before you can use the Move to iOS app. Follow the instructions on your iPhone’s setup screen to set up your device. Once you have completed the setup process, you can then open the Move to iOS app and start transferring your content.
