To take a picture of the document, align the document so that it fits completely in the frame of the camera, and use the focus and exposure controls located in the app you use for taking pictures. Once the desired image is captured, save it to a file.

Now, open an image editing tool to crop and straighten the image. This will improve the quality of the scanned document and make it easier to read. After the image is cropped and straightened, save the document to a different file in the desired format.

Finally, open the file in a PDF reader to check the quality of the scanned document. With an Android phone camera, you can easily turn paper documents into digital PDF files for further use.

2. Step by step guide on document scanning with Android phone camera#

Scanning documents is quite easy these days. With a modern Android phone, you can quickly scan documents with your camera. In this step-by-step guide, we will explain how to do it.

To start, open up the default camera app and point it at the document you want to scan. Once it’s in focus, press the shutter button and save the image. This should give you a clear and sharp document scan.

The next step is to crop the image to fit your purpose. Whether you want to scan a page from a book or a business card, cropping the image ensures that the document looks professional. Some camera apps offer advanced cropping options, so take advantage of them when you can.

Finally, you should adjust the brightness and contrast of the image. This will help the text stand out from the background and will make the document look more professional.

With these few simple steps, you can quickly scan documents with your Android phone camera and save them for later. It’s a great way to keep important documents within reach.
