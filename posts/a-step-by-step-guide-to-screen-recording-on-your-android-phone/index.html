Screen recording is a great way to capture your mobile activities, save your gaming achievements, and even use as evidence in an online dispute. Fortunately, it’s easier than ever to do on your Android phone. Here’s how:

First, you need to access the developer options on your phone. To open it, go to your device’s settings, select About phone, tap Build number seven times and then turn on the Enable OEM unlock option so that you can access the developer options.

Once you open the developer options, scroll down and find the Enable screen-recording option. There you can select the resolution, frame rate, and bit rate of the recording. Select the desired setting and click the Start button to begin the recording.

To stop recording, you can either tap the notification, or use the Q button to stop the recording. All your recordings will be saved in your phone’s gallery.
