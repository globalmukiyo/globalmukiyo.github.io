1. Preparing Your iPhone & Android Devices#

Before you begin the process of moving contacts from your iPhone 6 to your Android device, it is important to make sure that you have fully prepared your devices. There are five easy steps to get started:

First, back up all contacts from your iPhone 6 into iCloud. To do this, open the Settings app, tap ‘iCloud’, and then tap ‘Backup’. Select the toggle for ‘iCloud Backup’ and choose ‘Back Up Now’.

Next, ensure that you have logged into the same Gmail account on your Android device. To do this, open the Settings app, tap ‘Accounts’, and then tap ‘Add Account’. Enter your Gmail username and password, and then select ‘Sync Contacts’.

Third, open the Contacts app on your iPhone 6 and select ‘Groups’. Check the box next to ‘iCloud’ and select ‘Done’.

Fourth, open the Contacts app on your Android device. Tap the three-dot menu icon in the upper-right corner and then select ‘Import’. Select ‘iCloud’ and enter your Apple ID username and password.

The final step is to select which contacts you would like to import. Check the box next to each contact that you would like to move and select ‘Done’. After a few moments, your contacts from your iPhone 6 will be transferred to your Android device.
