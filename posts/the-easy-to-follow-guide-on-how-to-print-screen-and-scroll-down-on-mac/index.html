1. Steps to print screen on Mac#

Printing screen on Mac is easier than you thought. Here are the steps to print screen on Mac: First, press the “Command + Shift + 3” at the same time. This will capture a screenshot of the entire screen and the captured image will be saved to the desktop. Secondly, if you wish to capture a screenshot of a portion of the screen, press the “Command + Shift + 4” and select the area to capture. Once selected, the captured image will be saved to the desktop. Thirdly, if you wish to capture an entire scroll window, press the “Command + Shift + 4 + Space Bar” to capture a screenshot of the window. The captured image will be saved to the desktop. Finally, if you wish to capture just one particular part of a website, press the “Command + Shift + 5” and select the area to capture. The captured image will then be saved to the desktop. With these easy steps, you can now easily print screen and scroll down on Mac.

Simply place two fingers on the trackpad and move them in a downwards motion to scroll down. Finally, you can also use the scroll bar on the right side of your Mac screen to easily scroll down.
