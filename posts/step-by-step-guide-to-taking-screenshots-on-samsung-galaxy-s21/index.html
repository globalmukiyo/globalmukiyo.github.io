3. Tips for Taking Great Screenshots on Samsung Galaxy S21#

Taking screenshots on Samsung Galaxy S21 is a simple and quick process. First, make sure that you are on the screen that you want to take a screenshot of. Ensure that you have the right settings enabled in your device. You can do this by going to Settings > Advanced features > Screenshots and screen recorder. The settings will allow you to customize how the screenshot is taken and stored.

Third, if you want to record what is currently happening on your screen, you can use the Screen Recorder feature. To access this, just tap on the Screen Recorder icon in the Quick Settings panel. Set up the recording settings as per your requirements and then tap on the red record button to start recording.

Finally, you can edit the screenshot or video after taking it. Tap on the Image or Video icon in the notification panel to edit or share the screenshot. You can crop, add text or emojis, draw, crop, and much more to make your screenshot look perfect.

Q.1 How do I take a screenshot on Samsung Galaxy S21? A. To take a screenshot on your Samsung Galaxy S21, press and hold the power and volume down buttons together for a few seconds and the screenshot will be captured.

Q.2 How can I edit my screenshot on Samsung Galaxy S21? A. After capturing a screenshot, you can use the in-built editing options like cropping and drawing tools to edit the screenshot. To access the editing tools, tap on the screenshot to open it in the Gallery app.

Q.3 Can I take a long screenshot on my Samsung Galaxy S21? A. Yes, you can take a long screenshot or a ‘scroll capture’ on your Samsung Galaxy S21. To do so, press and hold the power and volume down buttons together for a few seconds, and then tap on the ‘capture more’ or ‘scroll capture’ option from the options that appear on your display.

Q.4 How can I share my screenshots on Samsung Galaxy S21? A. You can share your screenshots with other apps by tapping on the share icon from the bottom of the screenshot preview. You can also access the direct share option from the share icon to quickly share your screenshot with the people saved on your contact list.

Q.5 Where can I find my screenshots on Samsung Galaxy S21? A. The screenshots taken on your Samsung Galaxy S21 can be found in the gallery app. To access them, open your Gallery App and choose the ‘Screenshots’ folder from the list of folders. All your screenshots will be available in this folder.
