First, open the application window or page you want to screenshot. Press Shift-Command-4 simultaneously on your keyboard, and your cursor will turn into a crosshair. Move the cursor to select the portion you want to screenshot. Press the Space bar and the cursor will turn into a camera icon. Click the mouse or trackpad button, and you will have taken a screenshot.

Second, if you want to take a scrolling screenshot, press Shift-Command-5 simultaneously on your keyboard. Click the Capture Entire Screen option at the bottom of the menu. A menu will appear with various options. Click on the Capture Selected Window option and select the window you want to screenshot. When you are done selecting, click the Start Recording button.

Third, scroll down the page. When you are done scrolling, click the Stop Recording button, and you will have taken a scrolling screenshot. The screenshot is saved as a video file in .mov format. You can view it by clicking the thumbnail that appears in the corner of your screen.

Finally, you can view the screenshot and save it as a .png file. Right-click on the video, select Open with, and click Preview. You can edit the screenshot and save it as an image file in Preview. Then you can share it in any way you want.
