Here is a guide on how to restore your Android phone to factory settings using a PC.

First, install an Android SDK and connect the device to the PC with a USB cable. This will allow you to access your phone from your computer. Then, open up the Command Prompt on your machine and type “adb devices”. This will list all connected devices including your phone.

The next step is to use the command “adb backup -f C:/mybackup.ab”. This is the command to backup your entire device, which includes the system files, apps, photos, videos, and other data. Once the backup is complete, you can disconnect your phone from the PC.

Finally, reboot your device into recovery mode and select “Factory Reset”. This will erase all your data from your device and restore it to its original factory settings. Once the reset is complete, you can connect your device again and restore the backup file you created earlier.

By following the steps above, you can easily reset your Android phone to its factory settings using your PC. After that, you can sync all your data back to your phone for a fresh start.
