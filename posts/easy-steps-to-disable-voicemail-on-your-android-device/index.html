  1. Open your device Settings.

  3. Select ‘Call’ then ‘Call Rejection’.
  4. Tap ‘Auto Reject List’.
  5. The list of rejected numbers will appear.
  6. Tap the ‘+’ icon near the lower right corner.
  7. You will be asked to enter the number you want to block.
  8. Enter the voicemail number from the carrier, usually *86 or #646#.
  9. Tap ‘Add’.
  10. The voicemail service will now be blocked.

Q3: How to Re-Enable Voicemail on an Android Device? A: To re-enable voicemail on your Android device, go to the Settings app. Then, select ‘Network & Internet’ or ‘Phone’ depending on your Android version. Select ‘Call’ then ‘Call Rejection’. Tap ‘Auto Reject List’. The list of rejected numbers will appear. Tap the ‘x’ icon near the lower right corner to remove the voicemail number. Voicemail will now be re-enabled.
