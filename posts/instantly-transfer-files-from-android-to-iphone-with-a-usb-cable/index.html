1. How to Transfer Files From Android to iPhone via Cable#

Transferring files between two different operating systems can be a daunting task, but it doesn’t have to be. You can easily transfer files from an Android device to an iPhone using a USB cable. Once the two devices are connected, a prompt will likely appear on the iPhone. It will give you several options for the type of connection you want to use. Choose the Transfer Files (MTP) option and follow the on-screen instructions on the iPhone.

On the Android device, a pop-up will appear asking to Allow USB File Transfer. Tap on the check box to allow it and make sure to select the Always Allow option. This will enable you to quickly and easily transfer files from one device to the other in the future.

Once the setup is complete, you will be able to start transferring files from your Android device to your iPhone. To do this, simply select the files you want to transfer and drag them over to the iPhone.
