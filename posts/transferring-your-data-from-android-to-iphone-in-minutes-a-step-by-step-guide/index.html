2. Transferring Data from Android to iPhone#

Transferring data from an Android phone to an iPhone can be hassle-free if you know the right steps. To get started, you would need a compatible cable that comes with both your Android phone and your iPhone. Once your cables are connected, the transfer process is very simple.

Firstly, go to your Android’s Settings and tap “Backup and Restore”. This takes you to a page where you can select which data you wish to transfer. Tap on “Copy to iOS” and select the type of data you wish to transfer. You can select from a range of data such as photos, videos, music, contacts, messages, etc.

Once you have selected the data to transfer, the transfer begins and usually takes up to five minutes to complete the process. After the transfer is complete, a popup window will indicate that the data has been transferred successfully to your iPhone.

You can then disconnect the cable and your data will be transferred to your new phone. You can find it in the corresponding apps on your iPhone. For instance, photos transferred on the Android will be visible on the iPhone’s Photos app. This step-by-step guide of transferring data from Android to iPhone is a simple and quick process that only requires compatible cables and a few minutes of your time.
